Privacy policy

Privacy is the only thing that Loliful.io treats seriously.

We don’t ask for your personal information unless we truly need it. (Email, name and profile picture from social account)

We don’t share your personal information with anyone except to comply with the law, or protect our rights.

We don’t store personal information on our servers unless required for the on-going operations of one of our services.

Website Visitors

Like most website operators, Loliful collects non-personally identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Loliful’s purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Loliful’s visitors use its website. From time to time, Loliful may release non-personally identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

Loliful also collects potentially personally identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on its website. Loliful only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally identifying information as described below, except that blog commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog where the comment was left.

Gathering of Personally Identifying Information

Loliful will ask visitors to sign in before they cat start using the application. We use existing social networks as authorisation providers, and ask for following information: email, name and profile picture. We don’t store other information other than that.

Aggregated Statistics

Loliful may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors. However, Loliful does not disclose any personally identifying information.

Cookies

We don’t use cookies to track users, with the exception of using Google Analytics.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc.. Google Analytics uses cookies to help us analyse how users interact with our website. The information generated by cookies about your interaction with the website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers.

User content

Content, generated by our users, is saved on our servers and is not shared with third-parties.

Privacy Policy Changes

Although most changes are likely to be minor, Loliful may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in Loliful’s sole discretion. Loliful encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy.
